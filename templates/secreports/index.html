SecReports is an analytical domain that consolidates validated security reporting into actionable, time-bound insights on current threats for operational decision-making. It tracks cyber and physical security events, geopolitical triggers, sector-specific vulnerabilities, TTP evolutions, CVE disclosures and exploitation patterns, malware campaigns, and supply-chain incidents, with emphasis on near-term risk, exposure, and mitigations.

Subject Description

The workflow triages OSINT and commercial feeds with source reliability scoring, enriches and normalizes indicators, maps activity to MITRE ATT&CK techniques, and contextualizes CVE/CVSS data by environment to produce risk scores and trendlines. Clear alert thresholds and escalation criteria ensure signal-over-noise discipline, while concise narratives translate technical indicators into business impact.

Outputs include daily briefs and weekly risk roundups, IOC lists and watchlists, heat maps and timelines, and executive one-pagers that prioritize mitigations with effort-versus-impact guidance. Primary users include SOC and incident response teams, vulnerability management, risk leaders, and executive stakeholders who need timely, verifiable, and relevant threat visibility.
